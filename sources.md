## Rules

- Keep a small, trusted set — enough variety to triangulate, not so much it's noise.
- An exemplar doesn't need a registered source to be used; the registry just tracks your reliable wells.
- Note when a source stops being useful (moved, paywalled, went stale) rather than deleting the row.
